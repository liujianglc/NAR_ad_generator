About Outdoor Advertising

 

Production Quality: When images are enlarged for this format, a certain amount of film grain will result. This is normal and is not noticeable when the image is viewed at the distance from which outdoor advertising materials are intended to be seen.

Production Costs: Because outdoor advertising sizes vary by market, we must customize the size of the billboards and bus shelters by area. There is a small fee associated with this to cover studio time and shipping. Costs are as follows:

Outdoor Billboard: $300 each
Bus Shelter: $250 each
